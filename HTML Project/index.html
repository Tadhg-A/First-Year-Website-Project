Ocean Pulse Preservation Fund

The Ocean Pulse Preservation Fund is a focused and hardworking charity determined to improve conditions in all the oceans of the world. It's not hard nowadays to see the dangerous conditions that our ocean wildlife is forced to live in due to the carelessness and laziness of corporations and the greater human race. Not only has climate change caused disastrous effects on ocean environments, but also, due to illegal poaching, many species of fish are on the brink of extinction. One of the largest problems seen in the modern day, however, is the extreme pollution of the world's oceans by garbage and other dangerous substances.
